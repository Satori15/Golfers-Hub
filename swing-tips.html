Swing Tips & Drills

Simple drills and explanations to improve balance, tempo, and consistency.

Top 8 Swing Tips

  • Check your grip pressure — light, not tight.
  • Maintain a stable head position during the downswing.
  • Start the swing with the lower body.
  • Practice tempo with a metronome or count.
  • Use alignment sticks to ensure proper aim.
  • Work on short game — it saves strokes.
  • Record your swing to find repeatable errors.
  • Take lessons periodically to correct bad habits.

Drill: The Pause Drill

Take the club to the top, pause for one second, then continue through the swing. This helps train rhythm and transition.
